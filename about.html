About
Helping the Homeless
Two Socks at a Time

My name is Gavin and this holiday season I'm running a sock drive for the homeless in our wonderful Sacramento area, accepting new and gently used socks of all types. No money or cash donations. Sock Santa is not an established non-profit organization, however 100% of the proceeds are going directly to help our homeless community.

My goal is to raise 100 pairs of socks from November 1st through December 22nd. On December 25th, Christmas day, new unopened socks will be donated to homeless shelters around Sacramento and gently used socks will be handed out by me personally.
